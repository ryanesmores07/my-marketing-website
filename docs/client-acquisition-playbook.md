# Client Acquisition Operating Playbook

## Weekly Scorecard

- Review 20-40 ecommerce prospects.
- Send 10-20 specific, manual outreach messages.
- Improve or publish 1 authority asset, case-study detail, or landing-page proof point.
- Tag every inquiry by source, offer, budget, and quality.

## Prospect Fit

Prioritize ecommerce brands with at least one clear fit signal:

- Shopify store with Japan, cross-border, or bilingual market need.
- Weak localization, unclear product page, or poor trust cues.
- Missing or unclear GA4, Meta, Google Ads, or pixel/tracking setup.
- Active paid traffic going to a weak product page or landing page.
- Agency, consultant, or freelancer who could refer Shopify Japan work.

## Outreach Rule

Do not send broad cold pitches. Each message must include:

- One real observation from their site or market.
- One matching entry offer.
- One simple next step.

## Pipeline Stages

- Prospect
- Contacted
- Replied
- Qualified Call
- Proposal
- Won
- Lost
- Nurture

## 30/90 Day Review

- After 30 days: keep only sources producing qualified conversations.
- After 90 days: scale channels where lead cost and time cost make sense for under-$2k projects.
- Pause channels that produce unqualified leads, high acquisition cost, or no replies after enough testing.
